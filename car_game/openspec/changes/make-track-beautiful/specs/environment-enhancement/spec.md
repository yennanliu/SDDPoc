## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Decorative environment elements
The system SHALL render decorative elements (e.g., trees, lampposts) around the track.

#### Scenario: Environment elements are visible
- **WHEN** the game starts
- **THEN** various 3D models representing environment details are visible along the track boundaries

### Requirement: Improved lighting and shadows
The system SHALL support shadows for the car and environment elements to enhance depth.

#### Scenario: Shadows are cast on the track
- **WHEN** the car or decorative elements are in the scene
- **THEN** they cast realistic shadows onto the track surface and ground
